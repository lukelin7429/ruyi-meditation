Community · Dharma Events

Come up the mountain.

Throughout the year the doors of the monastery open for shared practice — and at the heart of it is our monthly retreat, a day set aside, every month, to climb the mountain and quiet the mind together.

Every month

The monthly retreat.

Once a month, practitioners make their way up to Ru-Yi — perched at 800 metres on Fengguidou mountain in Nantou's Xinyi Township — for a full day of practice together. It is the steady heartbeat of our community: come down from the busyness of the world, and spend a day doing nothing but turning toward the Dharma.

The day unfolds gently. We chant and recite the name of the Buddha together, sit in periods of quiet meditation, listen to a short teaching, and share a simple vegetarian meal. No experience is needed, and nothing is required of you but a willingness to be present. In winter the plum blossoms open white across the slopes and the mountain mist drifts through the courtyard — the setting itself does half the teaching.

The retreat is, like everything at Ru-Yi, completely free, and open to anyone who wishes to come.

The year on the mountain

Seasons of gathering.

Around the monthly rhythm, a handful of larger observances mark the turning of the year.

Spring · Vesak

Bathing the Buddha

浴佛節 · The Buddha's birthday

On the Buddha's birthday we pour fragrant water over the infant Buddha — an old and tender rite of purifying the heart, celebrated together in the main hall.

Summer · July

Youth & Children's Camps

學佛營 · A few days away from screens

Each summer the mountain fills with young people for a few free days of meditation, nature, stories, and character — the next generation learning to steady the mind.

Autumn · Ullambana

The Ullambana Assembly

盂蘭盆法會 · Gratitude to our ancestors

In the seventh lunar month we gather to remember and give thanks to our parents and ancestors — the festival of filial love and the transfer of merit.

Winter · New Year

New Year Homecoming

回娘家 · Returning home to the mountain

As the lunar year turns, old friends of the monastery come back up the mountain to begin the year in practice, blessing, and good company.

You are welcome

Join us for the next retreat.

Pick a date from the schedule above and simply come — no registration or experience is needed. If you would like help finding your way up the mountain, or want to confirm the latest timing, write to us and we will gladly point you the right way.
