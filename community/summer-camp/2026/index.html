Community · Summer Camp 2026

Two camps,
one mountain.

This July, Ru-Yi opens Fengguidou Mountain to two summer camps — one for children, one for teenagers. Both are free, both are taught in the spirit of the Dharma. Choose the one that fits.

A summer for every age.

The two programs share the same heart — kindness, gratitude, and a quieter way of being — but move at a different pace. The children's camp is playful and hands-on; the youth camp goes deeper, into filmmaking, meditation, and questions of character. Pick a camp below to see its full daily schedule and teaching team.

Ages 6–12 Children's Camp

The Little Bodhisattvas' Summer

兒童夏令營 · 小菩薩的夏天

Five playful days of stories, songs, crafts, cooking, and nature — the Buddha's teachings told the way a child can hold them. Gentle mornings, hands busy all afternoon, a closing day shared with families.

Youth Buddhist Study Camp

青少年夏令營 · 願力・覺察・共創・傳承

Four days built around one question — what kind of person do I want to become? Dharma classes, Buddhist micro-cinema, walking meditation, music, and a starlit film night.
