Youth Buddhist

Four days on Fengguidou Mountain — Dharma classes, filmmaking, music, walking meditation, and a starlit film night. July 4–7, 2026.

July 4–7, 2026 Fengguidou Mountain Teenagers · Free

Four days. Four themes.

This year's youth camp is built around a single question: what kind of person do I want to become? Over four days, teenagers explore that question through Dharma teachings, hands-on creativity, and quiet time in nature — filmmaking and Buddhist micro-cinema, morning chanting, moonlit meditation, music, and a closing gratitude letter written to someone they love.

Each day carries a theme. The four days together form an arc: from planting an intention, to learning to observe, to creating something together, to passing it on.
