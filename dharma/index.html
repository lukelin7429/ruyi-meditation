Dharma · 佛法

The Buddha's teachings, in plain English.

The Dharma is the whole body of the Buddha's teaching — the discourses he gave, the treatises the great masters wrote, and the methods of practice handed down for over two thousand years. We are gathering it here, one collection at a time, drawing on both the Theravāda and Mahāyāna traditions, and always in language an English speaker can actually use.
