The Heart of Love and Kindness

愛是一種情感：把善良種在心裡 · My Tree of Love 我的愛心樹
Principle  Right Speech · Inherent Light 正語·內在光明 English  “I love my ___ because…” · “An emotion is a feeling.” Level  Ages 5–16

This Week’s Path

Overview 本週重點

This week we look at the very root of kindness: love is an emotion that grows from the heart. We learn to turn the big, abstract word “love” into clear, caring actions — being generous, being respectful, and guarding our words so that trust can grow.

English Goal

英文目標

Tell the difference between an emotion and a feeling, and use the adjectives generous, respectful, nice. Practice the pattern “I love my ___ because…” and simple Subject + Verb + Object sentences like “I respect all people.”

Spiritual Goal

佛學／心靈目標

Understand that love is a clean emotion that rises from within. Practice Right Speech (正語) — never speaking badly of others behind their backs — and rest in the inherent light (內在光明), the bright Buddha-nature in every heart.

Sentence Patterns

句型 · Key Patterns
Pattern 1 · Saying why you love someone
I love my ___ because ___.
·I love my mom because she is kind.
·I love my grandpa because he listens to me.
·I love my friend because she is generous.
Pattern 2 · Defining a word
An emotion is a feeling.
·Love is an emotion.
·An emotion is a feeling in the heart.
Pattern 3 · Subject + Verb + Object
I ___ all people.
·I respect all people.
·I am generous, and I share with my friends.

Grammar Focus · Subject + Verb + Object & defining nouns

Many English sentences follow the order Subject + Verb + Object: I (subject) respect (verb) all people (object). To define a word, we use the verb is: An emotion is a feeling. Remember the article: a feeling, an emotion (use an before a vowel sound).

The Principle: The Heart of Love and Kindness

正語·內在光明 · Right Speech & Inherent Light

Love is not only a word we say — it is an emotion that rises from inside us, and it shows up in how we speak and act toward others.

Imagine that inside every person there is a small, warm light （內在光明）. The Buddha taught that this light — our bright, kind nature — is always there, even when we forget it. It never goes out.

How do we keep our light shining? One simple way is Right Speech （正語）. This means we do not say unkind things about people behind their backs. When we are respectful with our words, others feel safe with us, and trust grows. Trust is the strong root that makes a friendship — a relationship — last.

Being generous keeps the light bright too. Helping your grandparents, listening carefully when someone speaks, or sharing your spare coins are all small gifts of love (Dāna 布施). Love is an emotion, and kindness is what we do with it.

Check your understanding

  • What is the difference between an emotion and a feeling? 情感和感覺有什麼不同？
  • How does Right Speech help build trust? 「正語」如何建立信任？
  • What is your “inherent light,” and how can you keep it shining? 你的「內在光明」是什麼？要如何讓它持續發亮？

Let Your Light Shine 讓你的光明照耀

Right Speech and generosity are not rules from outside — they are the natural way our inner light wants to act. Every time you choose a kind word over an unkind one, or share instead of keeping everything for yourself, your tree of love grows a little stronger. Love begins in the heart, and kindness carries it into the world.

Practice: The Tree of Love

愛心樹 · Homework

Draw a large tree. On the branches, draw leaves or hearts and write English words for love and kindness. Color your tree beautifully! Choose the tier that fits you. 畫一棵大樹，在枝葉上寫下表示愛與善良的英文字，選一個適合自己的等級。

Beginner 初級組

5 words

Question 1 of 10
Which word means “慷慨的 / willing to give and share”?
Correct: C. Generous (慷慨的) describes someone who likes to give and share. Respectful = 尊重的; nice = 友善的.
Question 2 of 10
Complete the definition: “An emotion ___ a feeling.”
Correct: A. To define a single noun we use is: “An emotion is a feeling.”
Question 3 of 10
Choose the correct Subject + Verb + Object sentence.
Correct: D. English order is Subject (I) + Verb (respect) + Object (all people).
Question 4 of 10
Finish the pattern: “I love my brother ___ he helps me.”
Correct: B. Because gives the reason: “I love my brother because he helps me.”
Question 5 of 10
“Right Speech” (正語) means that we…
Correct: C. Right Speech means guarding our words — not gossiping or speaking badly of others — so that trust can grow.
Question 6 of 10
In this lesson, the “inherent light” (內在光明) is…
Correct: A. The inherent light is our Buddha-nature — the bright, kind heart that is always there and never goes out.
Question 7 of 10
Which word means “信任 / believing someone is safe and true”?
Correct: D. Trust (信任) is the strong root of a good relationship.
Question 8 of 10
Helping your grandparents or sharing your coins is an example of…
Correct: B. Giving and helping is generosity (Dāna 布施) — kindness in action.
Question 9 of 10
Choose the correct article: “Love is ___ emotion.”
Correct: C. Use an before a vowel sound: “Love is an emotion.” (Compare: a feeling.)
Question 10 of 10
What is the main lesson of “The Heart of Love and Kindness”?
Correct: A. Love rises from within; with Right Speech, respect, and generosity, we let that light shine and our tree of love grows.
