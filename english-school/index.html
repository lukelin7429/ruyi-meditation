Ru-Yi English School

English for the mind and the heart.

如意英文學校 · 線上複習課程

A bilingual curriculum that weaves English learning together with the cultivation of the heart. Each week brings vocabulary you can hear and say, sentence patterns, a story, a Buddhist principle, a daily practice, and a self-check quiz. Free, for our students and for seekers everywhere.

For our students: use these pages to review what we practiced in class — listen to the vocabulary, read the story again, and test yourself with the quiz. 上課的同學可以用這些頁面複習：聽單字發音、重讀故事、做小考檢測自己。
