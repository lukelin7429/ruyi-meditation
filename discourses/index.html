Part of the Khuddaka Nikāya, this collection gathers eighty short episodes from the Buddha's life, each closing with a spontaneous "heartfelt saying." Itivuttaka

Thus It Was Said

Part of the Khuddaka Nikāya, this collection gathers 112 short teachings, each following the same spare form: a prose statement opening "This was said by the Buddha," followed by a verse restating the same point. Arranged into four books by factor count — Ones, Twos, Threes, and Fours. Reading guide, full Sujato translation, and self-check quiz for every discourse.
