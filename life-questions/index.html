How to use this

From a problem to a practice.

Every theme is built the same way, so you can move from "I'm struggling with this" to "here's what I'll actually do" in one sitting.

1

Name what's hard

Pick the theme that matches what you're facing right now — not in theory, but today.

2

See what the texts say

Read how the Buddha and the great masters understood it, drawn straight from the discourses and treatises — in language you can use.

3

Take one thing to try

Each guide ends with a concrete practice — something to do, not just to think about — and links back to the source for going deeper.
