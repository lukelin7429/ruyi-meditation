Our mission

Life is short.

Ru-Yi Meditation Centre's mission is to provide people from all over the world with another path to happiness. In the modern age many people's lives are dominated by daily pressure, stress, work and concepts of success based around material possessions and status, often with negative effects on health, deep feelings of dissatisfaction and ultimately unhappiness.

Ru-Yi would like to offer a different way to define your own success and discover your own meaning in life — not defined by mainstream ways of thinking. We do this by sharing meditation skills and philosophies about the path to happiness. We offer free classes, accessible to all, and welcome anyone interested in learning how to use meditation to calm the mind and think about new purposes in life. Everyone is welcome to join our study groups focused on well-being.

Our dream at Ru-Yi is to create a supportive, welcoming community of meditation practitioners — to help improve the quality of our own lives and the lives of those around us. We bring fresh perspectives to current events and develop critical thinking to approach hot topics and share new ideas. We hope you will join us on this journey.
