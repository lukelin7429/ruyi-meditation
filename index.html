What we offer

Three ways to walk this path with us.

Ru-Yi means "as one wishes" — but the deeper meaning, drawn from iddhipāda, is to be master of one's own mind. These are the three doors we open.

Mental Clarity

心智清明

Practical techniques for moving beyond daily pressure and arriving at inner stillness. Meditation grounded in the original suttas, not pop psychology.

Community Support

社群陪伴

Small study circles, retreats in the mountains of Nantou, and a worldwide network of practitioners who keep each other honest on the path.

New Perspectives

嶄新視角

The Buddha's wisdom applied to the questions that actually trouble modern life: AI, family, career, climate, the news cycle, mortality.
