Ru-Yi Academy · 如意學院

Study the Dharma the way you'd take a course.

Not a library to browse, but a path to walk — guided courses that take you through the Buddha's teachings one text at a time, with a teacher, reading notes, real questions, and assignments. The way you'd learn anything worth learning.

The Buddha's Own Words

An Introduction to Buddhism through the Majjhima Nikāya

Most introductions to Buddhism hand you doctrines to memorize. This one has you read what the Buddha actually said. Over twelve sessions we walk through twelve carefully chosen discourses — arranged in learning order, from the most concrete and useful toward the more subtle — the way a college seminar reads a primary source.
